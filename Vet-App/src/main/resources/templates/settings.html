What is the settings page ?


The settings page is often left out of web projects. However, it is one of the essential templates when creating a service.

The settings page can group different types of features: account information, notifications, image format, offline mode, storage, version, disconnection, etc. Each service offers the necessary settings depending on the features of the the tool
